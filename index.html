About me

Welcome to my website!

My name is Daniel, a PhD-student affiliated with the National University of Singapore (NUS) and Genome Institute of Singapore (GIS). I'm fascinated by chromosomes and the interplay between genetic and epigenetic factors during cancer progression. Specific projects I'm working on include profiling copy number and histone modification heterogeneity in primary and progressed head and neck cancer (HNSC) with single-cell whole genome sequencing (scWGS) and single-nucleus CUT&RUN respectively and investigating the role of YAP1 amplification in mediating HNSC progression through novel cis- and trans-interaction. Please see my research page for more details.
